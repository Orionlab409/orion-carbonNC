Legal & Compliance: Our Commitment to Transparency

Terms of Service (Platform Use)

By accessing or using the Orion Carbon platform, you agree to these Terms. Our commitment to **Excellence** requires clear, unambiguous rules for participation.

1. Disclaimer of Financial Advice

Orion Carbon is a technology platform for carbon asset management and trading. We are **not** a financial advisor, broker, or investment firm. All decisions regarding the purchase, sale, or retirement of carbon credits are your sole responsibility.

2. Blockchain and Inherent Risk

The Platform operates on decentralized ledger technology. You acknowledge and accept the inherent risks of digital assets, including volatility, smart contract risks, and potential regulatory changes. The public nature of the blockchain upholds our principle of **Transparency** but does not eliminate risk.

3. Ownership and Token Integrity

Tokenized carbon credits on our Platform are certified and backed by verifiable MRV data, ensuring **Truth** in every asset. Ownership is immutable, recorded and transferred via smart contracts.

Privacy Policy (POPIA & Data Integrity)

We adhere to the principle of data minimization. Your privacy is protected through rigorous adherence to South African law and our internal standards of **Excellence**.

1. Compliance with POPIA

We are fully compliant with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa. We process, store, and secure personal information lawfully and responsibly.

2. Information Collected

We only collect data essential for compliance and service delivery:

  • Wallet Address (Used for platform identity and transaction records).
  • Email Address (Used for communication and optional newsletter subscription).
  • Aggregated Emissions Data (Non-personal data required for MRV and credit verification).

3. Data Sharing and Use

We do not sell or lease personal data. Information is shared only as required for:

  • Regulatory Reporting (as mandated by South African law).
  • Verification Processes (sharing MRV data with accredited third-party auditors).
  • Platform Operation and Security.

Regulatory Compliance (South Africa)

Our operation is built on deep expertise in the local regulatory environment, ensuring our clients achieve **Optimization** in their compliance strategies.

1. Carbon Tax Act & Carbon Budgets

Our development and trading services are structured to facilitate compliance with the South African Carbon Tax Act and the subsequent **Carbon Budget** regime. We ensure all credits generated and traded meet the required national standards.

2. World-Class MRV Standards

We utilize standardized, independently verified Measurement, Reporting, and Verification (MRV) protocols. This rigorous adherence to process establishes the **Truth** and integrity of every credit on our platform, critical for industrial accountability.
