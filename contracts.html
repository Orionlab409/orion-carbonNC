Contracts & Agreements: Clear and Compliant Engagement

Our world-class engagement process is governed by **Transparency** and legal **Excellence**, ensuring mutual clarity before project initiation.

Development Service Agreement (DSA)

For clients engaging us to develop and verify carbon credits from their emission-reduction projects.

  • Defines MRV Scope and Methodology (linked to **Truth**).
  • Outlines fee structure: Development Fee + Annual MRV Fee + Success Fee (10-20% of issued credits).
  • Establishes ownership rights of the resulting verified credits.

Platform Terms & Smart Contract

For users accessing our blockchain-enabled market for purchasing or retiring tokenized carbon assets.

  • Governs use of the platform and digital asset trading.
  • Includes the legal framework for the underlying smart contracts (enforcing **Transparency**).
  • Details platform fees, settlement procedures, and asset retirement rules.

Academy Training Contract

For youth bursaries, corporate upskilling, and agreements with placement partners.

  • Defines curriculum, duration, and certification standards (ensuring **Excellence**).
  • Outlines placement agreements for graduates joining the MRV field workforce.
  • Includes IP clauses for course materials and internal data protocols.
